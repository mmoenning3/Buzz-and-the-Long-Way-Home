Instructions

Help Buzz get to the Culc in time for class.

Move Buzz with your arrow keys.

However, there is a catch!

To win Buzz needs to pick up his A papers, so his GPA will increase. If he runs into the UGA bulldawg, Buzz will lose. If he hits any F papers his GPA will go down. Buzz cannot pass the grass barriers because shortcuts cause ruts!

So good luck crossing campus, and remember to stay off the grass.
